# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project expects contributors, maintainers, and community members to act
with professionalism and respect.

In this repository, that means:

- discuss technical disagreements directly and in good faith
- critique code, designs, and decisions, not people
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, discrimination, and intimidation
- do not spam, derail, or flood issues, discussions, or pull requests
- respect security boundaries, private reports, and other people's data

## Unacceptable behavior

The following is not acceptable in project spaces:

- personal abuse, threats, or sexualized language
- discriminatory or hateful language
- doxxing or publishing private information without permission
- bad-faith trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior
- repeated refusal to follow repository contribution and security rules

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, lock, hide, or remove comments, issues, discussions,
and pull requests that violate this policy. Repeated or severe violations may
lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

## Reporting

- For general conduct concerns, open a private maintainer contact through GitHub
  Discussions or Issues only if no private details are involved.
- For sensitive or security-related incidents, use the private reporting path in
  [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
